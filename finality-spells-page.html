ABOUT.

Finality spells is an addon for the popular Minecraft mod Irons Spells and Spellbooks. I've been working it as a way to display my art and animation in an interactive medium. It combines two of my favorite things- gaming and art- to create experiences that play as good as they look.

The goal of this mod is simple, add more spell casting enemies and challenging bosses to Iron’s Spells and Spellbooks, along with new spells to open new gameplay options.

A key feature of the mod is seen in a new type of spell, territory spells. There is only one for each school of magic, each changing how the player interacts with combat.
Above, you can see one of the territory spells, Advent.

This spell is assigned to the holy spell school and buffs spells from the same school while the player is within it’s area. It also grants a few instances of flight in its duration.

Though the project is not yet done, the progress I’ve made has taught me tons about my workflow and game design style. More than anything, i’m excited to see what I whip up next.
